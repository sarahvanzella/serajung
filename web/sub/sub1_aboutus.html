Pizza inglesina is a variety of traditional Italian pizza that is
topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and prosciutto crudo.
This last ingredient is a type of traditional Italian raw,
dry-cured ham as opposed to prosciutto cotto, which is cooked ham,
both of which are staples in Italian cuisine.
